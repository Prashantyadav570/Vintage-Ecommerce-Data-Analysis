### 📊 Ecommerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

### 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Ecommerce Sales Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and regional sales distribution.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights that enable stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify profitable products, evaluate customer contributions, and make informed business decisions.

---

### 🎯 Business Problem

An ecommerce company generates thousands of sales transactions every month across multiple products, customers, payment methods, and states.

Management wants answers to questions such as:

- Which state generates the highest sales?
- Which product category contributes the most revenue?
- Which sub-category generates maximum profit?
- Which customers generate the highest revenue?
- Which payment methods are most preferred?
- Which months generate profit or loss?
- Which quarter performs better?

Without an interactive dashboard, answering these questions requires manually analyzing thousands of records.

This dashboard provides a single place to monitor overall business performance.

---

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Track business profitability.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.

### 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales Overview

Provides an overview of overall business performance including:

- Total Sales
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Profit
- Average Order Value

---

### 2. Category-wise Sales Analysis

Compares sales quantity across product categories.

Categories include:

- Clothing
- Electronics
- Furniture

Business Use:

- Identify highest selling product categories.
- Improve inventory planning.

---

### 3. Monthly Profit Analysis

Displays monthly profit trends.

Business Questions Answered:

- Which months generated highest profit?
- Which months resulted in losses?
- Are there seasonal trends?

---

### 4. Customer Analysis

Shows top customers contributing to revenue.

Business Questions Answered:

- Who are the highest-value customers?
- Which customers should be targeted for loyalty programs?

---

### 5. Payment Mode Analysis

Analyzes customer payment preferences.

Payment Methods:

- Cash on Delivery (COD)
- UPI
- Debit Card
- Credit Card
- EMI

Business Use:

- Optimize payment options.
- Improve customer checkout experience.

---

### 6. Profit by Product Sub-Category

Compares profitability of different products.

Examples:

- Printers
- Bookcases
- Saree
- Accessories
- Tables

Business Questions Answered:

- Which products generate maximum profit?
- Which products need pricing improvements?

---

### 📌 Business Insights

The dashboard helps generate insights such as:

✅ Maharashtra contributes the highest sales revenue.

✅ Clothing is the highest-selling product category.

✅ Printers generate the highest profit among all sub-categories.

✅ COD is the most preferred payment method.

✅ Some months generate losses, indicating seasonal fluctuations.

✅ A small number of customers contribute significantly to total sales.
